Discussion

I think the key to implementing a unique, but balanced character focusing on excelling at skill checks in 4th-edition is a progression of feats.

As this will be their key characteristic, it is expected that they will be spending many feats on it. Skill Masters are masters of the general, and are not required (or expected) to take mundane.

This is pretty much my first look at such, and I haven't cross-referenced existing materials nearly as much as I should have. Assume that all feat/power names are supposed to be unique here - if they conflict with existing ones, that's a mistake, not a reference.

The 'Skill Master' progresses somewhat like multiclassing through feats - you're always *mostly* your primary class, but can have a significant amount of skill power as well.

The 'Skill Master' powers are controlled somewhat like the 'Channel Divinity' powers of many (all?) divine classes.

A Skill Master may not also be multi-classed.

A Skill master's 'implement' (like a Wizard's orb or Warlock's rod) is a Bag of Tricks. A standard Bag of Tricks may appear to be pretty much any mundane small container, has a market value of 200gp, and has an enhancement bonus of +1. For +2 and above, the cost is equivalent to a magic weapon of the same level. A Bag of Tricks applies its enhancement bonus to Skill Master powers with the 'Implement' keyword. As a 'typed' bonus it does not stack - i.e. it applies a maximum of once to a skill check, even where multiple Skill Master powers are involved.

It is intended that multiple Skill Master powers can be expended on the same skill check, based on the player's judgement of its importance.

Note that 'Preparation' and 'At The Ready' both grant a 'power' bonus to the check, and thus do not stack with each other.

Details

  1. Feat: Skilful
    Prerequisites: Level 4
    You gain access to the Skilful daily power.

    Skilful
    Feat Power
    Your dedication and determination drive you to success
    Daily
    Immediate Interrupt ♦ Personal
    Trigger: You would fail a skill check
    Effect: Add +5 feat bonus to the check result. This effect stacks with any initial +3 from the Skill Focus feat in the triggering skill.

  2. Feat: Skill Master (multiclass)
    Prerequisites: Skilful feat
    Your dedication to training and commitment to self improvement grants a great boon to your use of skills.
    You gain the Skill Master class feature, and three (3) uses of powers with the Skill Master keyword per day. You gain the Preparation and Bag of Tricks Skill Master powers.

    Preparation
    Skill Master Power
    Forethought and consideration aids your endevours
    At-Will ♦ Implement, Skill Master
    Free Action ♦ Personal
    Prerequisite: You have one minute to consider your course of actions.
    Effect: You gain a +5 power bonus to one skill check.
    This bonus increases to +10 at level 21.
    Special: If the nominated skill attempt is aborted before being attempted, this daily use of a Skill Master power is not expended.

    Bag of Tricks
    Skill Master Power
    I've got just the thing for this...
    At-Will ♦ Implement, Skill Master
    Free Action ♦ Personal
    Effect: Add your implement's enhancement bonus to any skill check

  3. Feat: Work Together
    Prerequisites: Skill Master class feature
    You gain the Work Together Skill Master power.

    Work Together
    Skill Master Power
    You can work effectively as master or student, and inspire competence and cooperation in others.
    Encounter ♦ Skill Master
    Move Action ♦ Ranged 5
    Effect: When working with up to four allies to perform a skill at least one of you are trained in, the roll DC for all assisters is reduced by 2.
    At level 11: If you are trained and performing the skill, the bonus from each ally assisting you increases to +3 (maximum +12)
    At level 21: If you are trained and performing the skill, the bonus from each ally assisting you increases to +4 (maximum +16)

  4. Feat: Skill Master Focus
    Prerequisites: Skill Master class feature
    You gain two additional uses of Skill Master powers per day.
    This feat can be taken multiple times, each time granting two more uses of Skill Master powers per day.

  5. Feat: Skill Haste
    Prerequisites: Skill Master class feature
    You gain the Skill Haste Skill Master power.

    Skill Haste
    Skill Master Power
    Anything you can do, I can do faster
    Encounter ♦ Skill Master
    Free Action ♦ Personal
    Effect: A single skill check that normally takes a full-round action or less to complete is reduced in duration by one action.
    Full-round -> Standard -> Move -> Minor -> Free

  6. Feat: Skill Perseverance
    Prerequisites: Skill Master class feature
    You gain the Skill Perseverance Skill Master power.

    Skill Perseverance
    Skill Master Power
    You can maintain intense focus for incredible periods
    Encounter ♦ Skill Master
    Free Action ♦ Ranged 5
    Effect: You reduce the time taken for yourself or an ally to perform a lengthy (two rounds or more) skill check by half.

  7. Feat: At The Ready
    Prerequisites: Skill Master class feature, level 11
    You gain the At The Ready Skill Master power.

    At The Ready
    Skill Master Power (11)
    A lifetime of preparation separates you from the pack
    At-Will ♦ Implement, Skill Master
    Minor Action (x3) ♦ Personal
    Prerequisite: You spend three minor actions to consider the challenge ahead.
    The actions can be spent in up to 5 rounds, and need not be successive.
    Effect: You gain a +5 power bonus to one skill check.
    Special: If the skill attempt is aborted before being attempted, this daily use of a Skill Master power is not expended.

  8. Feat: Every Trick in the Book
    Prerequisites: At The Ready feat, level 21
    You gain the Every Trick in the Book Skill Master power.

    Every Trick in the Book
    Skill Master Power (21)
    No challenge is new to you
    Daily ♦ Skill Master
    Free Action ♦ Personal
    Effect: You gain an additional +5 bonus to a use of your Preparation or At The Ready power

  9. Feat: Do or Do Not
    Prerequisites: Skill Master class feature, 4 Skill Master powers, level 28
    You gain the Do or Do Not Skill Master power.

    Do or Do Not
    Skill Master Power (28)
    There is no try.
    Daily ♦ Skill Master
    Free Action ♦ Personal
    Effect: Every skill check you make for one minute succeeds.
